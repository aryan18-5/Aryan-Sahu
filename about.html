Gurukul Kurukshetra

Gurukul Kurukshetra is run by Arya Pratinidhi Sabha, Rohtak Haryana. A twelve members management committee is constituted for three years to efficiently manage the affairs of Gurukul. Gurukul does not get any financial aid or grant from the government. Free of cost education is provided to the students hailing from 18 states of India and abroad. Gurukul Management Committee arranges funds through its own resources for various purposes. Under the patronage of His Excellency Governer Gujarat, Gurukul Management Committee has taken various initiatives in the field of nature farming.
